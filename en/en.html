About Holiday Stay Nika

At Holiday Stay Nika, located in Busk, guests have access to a garden, shared lounge, terrace, and free Wi-Fi. Additional amenities include room service, a restaurant, and a children’s playground. Free private parking is also available. An airport shuttle service is provided at an additional cost.

All rooms at Holiday Stay Nika are equipped with a wardrobe. Rooms have a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, and offer views of the garden. All rooms come with a flat-screen satellite TV.

Guests can enjoy table tennis at Holiday Stay Nika.

The 24-hour reception staff speaks both English and Ukrainian.
